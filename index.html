About P.L. Deshpande

Purushottam Laxman Deshpande, fondly known as PuLa, was an iconic figure in Marathi literature. His wit, humor, and storytelling charm have left an indelible mark on Indian literature and arts.

Notable Works

Vyakti Ani Valli

A collection of character sketches capturing the humor and quirks of Marathi society.

Batatyachi Chaal

Satirical masterpiece portraying the common man's life.

Asa Mi Asa Mi

An autobiography reflecting on his life with humor and wisdom.

Legacy

P.L. Deshpande's legacy continues to inspire generations of writers, artists, and fans who cherish his humor and insight into human nature.
